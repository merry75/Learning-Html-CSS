The Beginning 13/12/2016

Let's start with about who am I ? My name is Benjamin Abitbol, i'm a 27 years old male from Paris with a master degree from an average business school "so to speak". I recently made my alliyah in Israel a few months ago with the hope of a new beginning and a bright future. Yet here am I today struggling to find a job in this new country. It has now been two months I have been sending hundreds of resume to companies, passing dozens of interviews without success until I decided no more... No more of being dependent from the society's standards which according to their logic you should follow those steps :

  • go to university/colleges
  • get a degree
  • apply and find a job

What if you were able to chose the things you want to do in your life whenever you god damn please ? Well it certainly didn't take me 27 years to realize that but to act upon it this is a different story. I guess you can call this day, the Beginning of me taking actions toward my future and not just think about endless possiblities just to remember 2,3 years later "if...if...if.." and live with regrets.

My Plan

When it comes to making money online there are so much possibilities that you don't even know where to start. Websites ? Advertising ? Blogging ? Affiliate ? and blabla the list goes on and on... The worst in all that ? If you are a complete noob/beginner such as me, you end up totally lost and just think about what you should and should not do. This is the process that usually everyone is confronted with:
  1. Shitload of information
  2. learning
  3. way too much complicated back to square 1
  4. learning again
  5. take action (MOST IMPORTANT ONE where i am standing right now!)
  6. keep learning while taking action
  7. encounter failures
  8. finally succeed and one day voila you are an Expert at what you do!

How i will make it happen ?
